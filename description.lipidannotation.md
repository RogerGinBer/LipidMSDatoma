## Introduction

LipidMS 3.0 is designed for untargeted LC-MS/MS-based lipidomics, offering a comprehensive data analysis workflow from pre-processing to lipid annotation. The package has been enhanced to include data-dependent acquisition (DDA) and additional lipid classes. In a comparison with other tools, LipidMS 3.0 demonstrates similar pre-processing performance to XCMS, and it complements annotations from MS-DIAL, providing more structural information with fewer incorrect annotations.
